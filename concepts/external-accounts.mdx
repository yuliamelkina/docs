## Overview

External accounts represent your customers' financial accounts (bank accounts, payment accounts) that can be used as destinations for fiat withdrawals.


## How External Accounts Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Account Registration">
    Customer provides bank account details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validation & Verification">
    Omniwire validates account information and performs compliance screening
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account Approval">
    Approved accounts become available for withdrawal
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Account Types

### US Bank Accounts

US customers can add checking and savings accounts using routing and account numbers:

**Required Information:**
- Bank name and account name
- Account holder's full name and address
- Routing number (9 digits)
- Account number
- Account type (checking or savings)

### Mexican Accounts (MXN)

Mexican customers can add Peso-denominated accounts:

**Required Information:**
- Bank name and account details
- Account holder information  
- Mexican address verification


## Account Information Security

### Sensitive Data Handling

Omniwire implements strict security measures for financial account information:

- **Full Account Numbers**: Never returned in API responses - only last 4 digits shown
- **Encryption**: All account details encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Access Control**: Account details only accessible to authorized customer and tenant
- **Audit Logging**: All account access and modifications logged for compliance


## Compliance & Security Features

### Automatic Compliance Screening

All external accounts undergo comprehensive compliance screening:

- **OFAC Sanctions Screening**: Account holders checked against sanctions lists
- **AML Risk Assessment**: Account patterns analyzed for money laundering risk
- **PEP (Politically Exposed Person) Screening**: Enhanced due diligence for high-risk individuals
- **Bank Validation**: Institution verification and regulatory status checking

### Data Protection

Omniwire implements bank-grade security for external account data:

- **End-to-End Encryption**: Account details encrypted throughout the entire system
- **Zero-Knowledge Architecture**: Full account numbers never logged or cached
- **Secure Access Controls**: Role-based permissions for account access
- **Regular Security Audits**: Continuous monitoring and penetration testing

### Regulatory Compliance

External account management meets strict regulatory requirements:

- **PCI DSS Compliance**: Payment card industry data security standards
- **SOC 2 Type II**: System and organization controls audit certification  
- **GDPR Compliance**: European data protection regulation adherence
- **Bank Secrecy Act**: US anti-money laundering regulation compliance
