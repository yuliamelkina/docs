## What is Omniwire?

Omniwire is a financial infrastructure platform that bridges traditional banking and cryptocurrency ecosystems. Our APIs enable businesses to seamlessly onboard customers, manage custodial crypto wallets, and facilitate conversions between fiat currencies and digital assets through automated virtual accounts and transfer orchestration.

## Core Capabilities

### Customer Onboarding & KYC
Create customers with comprehensive identity verification, document collection, and compliance screening. Manage endorsements for different payment rails and track verification status through automated workflows.

### Multi-Chain Custodial Wallets  
Generate and manage custodial wallets across Solana, Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, and other major blockchain networks. Handle private key security, transaction signing, and real-time balance tracking automatically.

### Virtual Banking Accounts
Provide customers with unique ACH and Wire account details that automatically convert incoming fiat deposits to cryptocurrency and deliver to specified wallet addresses.

### Cross-Rail Transfers
Move funds between traditional banking systems (ACH, Wire, SEPA) and blockchain networks with automatic currency conversion, optimal routing, and comprehensive fee transparency.

## Popular Integration Patterns

### Crypto Onramp Services
Enable customers to convert fiat currency to cryptocurrency through ACH or Wire transfers. Automatically deliver converted crypto to custodial wallets across multiple blockchain networks.

### Digital Asset Offramp
Allow customers to convert cryptocurrency holdings back to fiat and withdraw to their verified bank accounts through automated liquidation addresses or direct transfers.

### Multi-Chain Wallet Infrastructure  
Provide custodial wallet services across Solana, Ethereum, and other networks with secure key management, balance tracking, and transaction orchestration.

### Compliant Crypto Business
Build KYC-compliant cryptocurrency services with automated customer verification, document collection, and regulatory reporting capabilities.
